Forest Trails is a residential subdivision of 205 homes and is located in Fairwood; a 6.2-square-mile, unincorporated community and census-designated place (CDP) in King County that falls under the political and administrative jurisdiction of the city of Renton, Washington.

As a common interest community, Forest Trails is managed by the Forest Trails Homeowners' Association that is made up of an all-volunteer, community-elected Board of Directors.

Our community is located near several main arterials, is minutes from a major hospital facility, UW Medicine Valley Medical Center, and has its educational needs served by the Kent School District. Our entrance is located at cross street SE 192nd Street and 133rd Avenue SE.
